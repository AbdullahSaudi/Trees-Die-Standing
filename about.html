Trees Die Standing Project

"Trees die standing" - that's the title of the most renowned work of Alejandro Casona, and now due to the climate change, we can see it with our own eyes, and it's happening at an alarming rate.

Our team, comprising of people with a keen passion to work for the environment, is planning to deeply analyze this situation and try to take a chance at solving this pressing problem.

We are trying to solve the problem of the negative impact on forests by educating people and helping them know their role and cooperating to make the world a better place.
